We are passionate about music, people and their creations. Creators of dreams with intriguing mindsets and a vision that unites us. These are the people that we want to be close to and collaborate with.

Not My Tempo was born in Lugano, in the Southern part of Switzerland, a place that draws together European influences between north and south.

We are still figuring out what NMT is going to be and what it will end up standing for. While we work this out, follow us and listen to our series of playlists specifically created for NMT.

Whenever you feel a connection, do reach out and define NMT with us.
